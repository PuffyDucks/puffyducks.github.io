2002 State of the Union Address

George W. Bush's 2002 State of the Union Address

  • On Tuesday, January 29, 2002, president George W. Bush delivered his 2002 State of the Union Address.
  • He claimed that North Korea was a "regime arming with missiles and weapons of mass destruction while starving its citizens."
  • During this point, there was no solid evidence of North Korea creating nuclear weapons and intentionally starving citizens.
  • This led him to cut all deals with North Korea, previously developed by Bill Clinton.
